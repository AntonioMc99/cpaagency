What We Do

Our Services

From personal escorts to private security details — CPA agents are certified, trained, and ready to serve your every need with professionalism and integrity.

🚗

Personal Escorts

Safe, professional escort services for loved ones, children to and from schools, dates, and more. Wherever they need to be — we get them there with the utmost professionalism.

✈️

Airport Transportation

All CPA agents are cleared for private and public airport pickups and drop-offs. Includes Executive Courtesy Lifts — parents ride FREE. We also have a certified Executive Private Pilot for in-state flights.

🛡

Private Details

Custom agent teams from 1 to 100 agents at your service. Door opening, area clearing before your arrival — full freedom to get things done at your request.

👊

Bodyguards

All CPA agents are professional at all times — not personal bullies. Bodyguards ensure no one is in close proximity to you, with strong situational awareness at all events.

🔒

Security Guards

Unarmed, armed, and PPO officers available to assist in any capacity. Serving families, businesses, and community events with flexible coverage that fits your needs.
