What is C.P.A?

Certified Protection Agency is an Executive Protection Service that focuses on the higher-end client's needs on a day-to-day basis — covering all aspects of private care including escort, bodyguard, private details, jewelry pickup/drop-off, document transport, and more.


We focus on building and earning your TRUST while showing the utmost INTEGRITY to every client. CPA works best by being fully TRANSPARENT — and we take pride in providing services for families, businesses, and communities that are part of our tomorrow and future.
